## 用途
Tests if the target's target is within a certain distance.


## 細項設定

| 技能名稱 | 簡化寫法| 用途 | 預設值 |
|-----------|-----------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|---------|
| distance  | d | Distance to check   | 0   |


## 範例
```yaml
  Conditions:
  - targetwithin{d=10} true
```
