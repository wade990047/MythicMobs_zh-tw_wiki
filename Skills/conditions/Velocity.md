## 用途
Checks the velocity of the target entity against a range


## 細項設定
| 技能名稱 | 簡化寫法| 用途 | 預設值 |
|-----------|-----------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|---------|
| velocity  | v | The velocity to check for   | |


## 範例
```yaml
  Conditions:
  - velocity{v=<3} true
```
