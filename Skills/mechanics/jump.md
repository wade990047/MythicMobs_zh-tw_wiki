Mechanic: Jump
==============

Causes the mob to jump with the given velocity. Using the velocity
*0.75* will make the mob jump approximately 1 block high. Can be a
negative number, too.

Attributes
----------

| 技能名稱 | 簡化寫法| 用途 | 預設值 |
|-----------|---------|--------------------------------|---------------|
| velocity  | v   | The velocity of the mob's jump | 1 |

  

Examples
--------

SuperJump:
  Skills:
  - jump{velocity=20}
